ABOUT ME
"The best doctor is the one you run to and can't find"

The website provides a convenient way for patients to book appointments for consulting a doctor according to their preferences.

Doctors can register by providing necessary details regarding specialization, location, time slots available for consultation at each location, maximum number of appointments at each location and a password for logging in later. Once registration is complete, the doctor will be provided a unique DoctorID which is to be used along with the password to log and make any modifications to their prior entries. The details of a registered doctor will be available to the patient for booking appointments. The patient has the option to choose from a variety of search criteria, namely, doctor name, specialization, location, to find a doctor and book appointments. Upon fixing the appointment, the user will be provided with a booking ID which is to be produced 30min before the allotted time at the appropriate location to confirm the appointment.
